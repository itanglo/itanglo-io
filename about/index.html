About Me

I'm Paolo Di Pasquale, a web developer from Italy 🇮🇹, living in England 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿. I grew up in a little coastal town in Sicily before moving to a village on the River Thames in Oxfordshire in the 90s.

Today, I'm a Staff Engineer on Team Web at Intercom where we build and maintain the company marketing websites intercom.com and fin.ai.

As a kid, I remember playing on a Commodore 64, especially during the long afternoons, waiting for my parents to take me to the beach. When I got a bit older my uncle let me use his Amiga 500 💾, which I still mainly used for playing games 🕹️, but I also discovered Deluxe Paint 2 and spent countless hours reproducing my favourite football players in pixel form.

In my spare time I like to take hikes in the countryside, do a bit of yoga, play board games with my family, read actual books and tinker with this website. I think you'll find an assortment here of work related stuff, photos of walks, trips away and links to things I find interesting around the web. Thanks for dropping by.
